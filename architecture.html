(Data-)Enterprise Architecture Management

The project develops an (Data-) Enterprise Architecture Management for the fictional company DataWise Vision, focusing on data process redesign. It starts with an overview of the company and its business model. A SWOT analysis and process mapping assess the current state. The project then identifies improvements, evaluates data and technology architectures, and plans for migration to the new architecture. Governance and change management strategies are also outlined for implementation and future updates.
